About Me

Hi I'm Dominique, and I'm software developer at Dominion Energy. I really love what I do, but it definitely wasn't the plan. Throughout my life I always knew I'd be a medical doctor.

After graduating premed with a BS in Chemistry, I took a year off to work in a lab and study for the MCAT. While at the lab I caught the coding bug and ended up spending most of my free time developing new and efficient ways of recording and handling data.

My work at the lab landed me in the IT department where I've been learning and growing my skill set ever since. I'm super excited to be in this Bootcamp, to get the opportunity to tackle new technologies and officially become a full stack developer.
